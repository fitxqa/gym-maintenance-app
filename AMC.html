Terms & Conditions

1. Scope of Services

  • Preventive Maintenance: Regular scheduled maintenance visits as per selected frequency including cleaning, lubrication, calibration, and safety checks.
  • Breakdown Maintenance: Emergency repair services for equipment failures during contract period.
  • Comprehensive AMC: Includes all spare parts (except consumables), labor, and transportation costs.
  • Non-Comprehensive AMC: Includes labor and service charges only; spare parts charged separately at actuals.

2. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

  • FitX shall respond to breakdown calls within the agreed response time (4/8/24/48 hours).
  • Resolution time commences from the time of site visit confirmation.
  • Delays caused by non-availability of client representative or power supply shall not be counted.
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, strikes) exempt SLA compliance.

3. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide safe working environment and access to equipment during scheduled visits.
  • Ensure proper power supply (230V ± 10%, 50Hz) and grounding.
  • Maintain equipment cleanliness between service visits.
  • Report breakdowns immediately through designated helpline.
  • Not attempt self-repair or engage third-party technicians without FitX consent.
  • Provide accurate equipment inventory and installation details.

4. Exclusions & Limitations

  • Damage due to misuse, negligence, or unauthorized modifications.
  • Consumable items (belts, bearings, cables, upholstery) unless specified in comprehensive AMC.
  • Cosmetic damage, scratches, or aesthetic wear and tear.
  • Equipment operated beyond rated capacity or specifications.
  • Vandalism, theft, or fire damage.
  • Software/firmware issues in electronic consoles (unless hardware-related).

5. Payment Terms

  • AMC fees payable as per selected payment schedule.
  • Services may be suspended after 15 days of payment default.
  • All payments via bank transfer/cheque only; cash payments not accepted above QR 10,000.

6. Contract Duration & Termination

  • Contract valid for selected period from start date.
  • Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.
  • Pro-rata refund applicable only for termination by FitX without cause.
  • No refund for client-initiated termination after 30 days of commencement.
  • Automatic renewal unless 60 days prior notice given by either party.

7. Liability & Insurance

  • FitX liability limited to AMC value or actual repair cost, whichever is lower.
  • Client responsible for insuring premises and equipment against fire, theft, and natural calamities.
  • FitX technicians covered under applicable labor laws.
  • FitX not liable for business interruption losses or consequential damages.

8. Dispute Resolution

  • Disputes resolved through mutual negotiation first.

9. General Conditions

  • This contract constitutes entire agreement; supersedes all prior discussions.
  • Amendments valid only if in writing signed by both parties.
  • Neither party may assign rights without prior written consent.
  • Severability: Invalid provisions don't affect remaining contract validity.
  • Force Majeure: Neither party liable for failures beyond reasonable control.

10. Special Conditions

  • Equipment older than 7 years or out of manufacturer warranty requires separate assessment.
  • AMC coverage suspended if equipment moved to different location without intimation.
